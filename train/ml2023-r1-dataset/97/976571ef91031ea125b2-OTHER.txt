/ - steady growing
\ - steady decreasing
⇒ - horizontal range
^ - break ASC-to-DESC
v - break DESC-to-ASC
╛ - range exit upward
╕ - range exit down
< - expanding range
> - narrowing range
⇗ - growing range
⇘ - decreasing range
? - cannot determine
E - error

D - last 24h (day)
W - last week
M - last 30d (month)
Q - last 3 months
Y - last 360d (year)

Price - 24h AVG price
Δ/D   - Ratio of current
        AVG price to
        prev. day AVG price
Δ/M   - Ratio of current
        AVG price to
        prev. month AVG price

* - newbie on the market,
    a coin that has been trading
    for less than a year
