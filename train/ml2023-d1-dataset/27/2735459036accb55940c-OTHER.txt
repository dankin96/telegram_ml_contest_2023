Any request related to transfer of students will be entertained until 12:00 PM on October 19, 2023. However, please note that those who would like to join AAiT through transfer should fulfill the following minimum requirements. 

👉🏿 Should not have an "F", "NG" or "I" grade. 

👉🏿 Must have attended and passed the following two courses: Applied Mathematics - I (Math 1041) and General Physics (Phys 1011). 

👉🏿 At the end of his/her first year should have a minimum CGPA of 3.5.
